Who are you?

I'm Matt Wilson. That's why that name is in the name of the website. Try to keep up.

Why do you do what you do?

I love the challenge of UX design. To me, thinking like a designer is like working on a puzzle, and creating a satisfying user experience is like putting the final piece in place.

What are you like?

I am a very conscientious and empathetic person, which I think are the most important qualities for any designer to have. I'm also a quick learner and a smart worker who has a pleasant personality and a good sense of humor. Or at least that's what I'd like to think.

How much money do you have in your bank account?

Glad you asked! Not a lot. Very little, actually. Please hire me.
