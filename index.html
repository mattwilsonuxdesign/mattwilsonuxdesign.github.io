PORTFOLIO

Take a look at some stuff I did

Digital Meal Kit Instructions

Digital meal kit assembly instructions that I designed and wrote the logic for using the manufacturing app platform Tulip. This design went through several iterations and testing phases and it was when I learned that UX design was my passion. Every aspect of the design from the language of the instructions, to the font size, to the actions that could be performed at each stage of the process, had to be well thought out with a specific end user in mind. I loved coming up with creative ways to solve potential problems our user might have. This research and foresight led to a successful rollout of our product.

Food Tech Website

A B2B website I created for a meal kit company with the use of Squarespace. My favorite challenge of this design process was creating a design that was consistent with the branding of the company's original website while still being a unique experience tailored to a different end user.

Financial Advisor Website

A website I created for a client. This was the first time I had worked with a client so it was the first time that I really dove right into and learned to love the design process.
